Whether it’s for a short video on social media or something longer, I enjoy every part of the process.

I also enjoy creating content about random fascinating facts. I love learning new things, and when I find interesting facts, I feel excited to share them with other people. Example: did you know honey never spoils? Archaeologists found pots of honey in ancient tombs that are still edible!

I also do blogging, side-scrolling gaming, and discovering phone & computer tips and tricks, and I enjoy sharing these finds with others.
